About

Founded in 1952 by Jason's father, Virgil Amburn, Amburn’s Market has been a cornerstone of our community for over 70 years. We are passionate about supporting local vendors and farmers, bringing you the freshest produce, farm-fresh eggs, milk, and cheese, as well as high-quality local meats, including beef, pork, lamb, and chicken. We specialize in carrying in-season South Carolina strawberries and truck loads of peaches. Our shelves are stocked with homemade desserts, fresh-baked local breads, Amish goods, and so much more. At Amburn’s Market, we have everything you need to celebrate the seasons in style. In the fall, you’ll find local décor like pumpkins, corn stalks, straw bales, and hardy mums — all perfect for creating a warm, welcoming touch. As the holidays approach, we carry fresh Christmas trees, wreaths, and garland to fill your home with the fragrance and beauty of the season. We also create custom gift baskets and fruit baskets and offer a wide selection of old-fashioned candy and nuts, making it easy to find something special for everyone. At Amburn’s Market, we believe in community, and we love ours! Thank you for letting us serve you!
