About

Amburn’s Market specializes in fresh fruits and vegetables, meat, dairy, baked goods, and other locally sourced products. We are a continuation of a legacy built by Jason’s father, Virgil, who began operating in our community in 1952, selling fruit out of the trunk of his car. "We are proud to continue the tradition of our family business and to offer the community access to high-quality, locally sourced food products. "Our goal is to provide a friendly, welcoming atmosphere where our customers can shop for fresh, whole food items while enjoying the kind of hometown service that has been a hallmark of our family business for generations."


Our Values

We are committed to supporting local farmers and producers, and we are working with a network of suppliers to ensure that our products are locally sourced when available. Amburn's Market desires to continue to be a staple in the Blount County community, offering a a range of local coffee, baked goods, including bread, pastries, and other treats, and a warm and welcoming atmosphere. With a commitment to community involvement and hometown service, we would love to become your favorite fresh food destination.
